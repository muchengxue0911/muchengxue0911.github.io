Research Interests

My research interests primarily lie in the fields of computer vision and pattern recognition, with a specific focus on advancing technologies and methodologies in semantic segmentation, point cloud analysis, referring expression grounding, and video object segmentation under various supervisory conditions, including weak, semi, unsupervised, and continual learning environments, as well as domain-shifting and long-tailed scenarios. My comprehensive approach not only explores the theoretical underpinnings but also aims to enhance practical applications such as anomaly detection, person re-identification, and image super-resolution.
